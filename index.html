About Us

RPS IT & MANAGEMENT PVT. LTD. Regd. Under Ministry Of Corporate Affairs, NCT New Delhi, MSME, ISO CERTIFIED 9001:2015, Government of India. It is only to make Information Technology as a basic knowledge to people of Rural & Urban area. Working in different fields of programme & Commercial training conducted by the State Government & Central Government to approach every class of the society. RPS is working across whole nation with Various Authorized Study Center (ASC) and a wide network in many states of the country. The main function of the society is to provide higher technical education in nominal charges for every group of society of Urban & Rural areas all over India and get success in computer revolution which is the main dream of Indian Govt. In present time, some big Institution run their one-year or more than one-year programme in higher charges. Due to which the middle class families of our societies cannot afford the load of their charges. Our mission studied their theme properly and decided to provide better higher technical education in computer. The RPS team regularly works to connect the society to the IT sector.
